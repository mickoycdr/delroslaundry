About Us

delro's Laundry is a rapidly growing chain of laundromats with a focus on providing a clean, comfortable, and convenient alternative to traditional coin-operated laundromats. Our stores feature state-of-the-art machines, soft water, air conditioning, video monitoring, and so much more. We offer coinless laundry options and convenient services like WASH/DRY/FOLD and in select locations, a PICK-UP & DELIVERY LAUNDRY SERVICE where we do your laundry for you!

delro's Laundry opened its first store in 2023. We work hard to redefine the laundromat experience through top-notch service, innovative technology, and—of course—by maintaining the cleanest and friendliest laundromat in town.
